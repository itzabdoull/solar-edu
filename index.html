Who this is for

Built for both academic learning and practical decision-making.

This website balances explanation and application. A beginner can use it to understand the science of photovoltaic conversion. A school project team can use it to plan a basic model system. A homeowner or business operator can use it to think through energy demand, storage, and monthly bill reduction.

Because solar adoption is shaped by local electricity prices, reliability issues, climate, shading, maintenance, and financing, the site explains where solar performs well, what limits it, and what assumptions must be checked before investing.

Study sequence

  1. Start with energy concepts and solar terminology.
  2. Learn how modules, inverters, controllers, and batteries interact.
  3. Estimate loads and compare system architectures.
  4. Evaluate economics, policy, maintenance, and failure risk.
  5. Advance into storage strategy, grid support, and performance analysis.
