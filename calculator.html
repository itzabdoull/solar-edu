This estimate assumes the chosen percentage of your monthly energy use is covered by solar. Real results depend on weather, load timing, battery storage, equipment quality, and system design.

How to interpret it

Use the result as a teaching estimate, not a purchase decision by itself.

If your result looks very attractive, the next question is whether the required system size, battery capacity, and installation budget are realistic. High savings often imply a larger system and therefore higher upfront cost.

If your result looks modest, solar may still be useful if it reduces generator use, protects critical operations during outages, or stabilizes electricity quality for sensitive devices.
